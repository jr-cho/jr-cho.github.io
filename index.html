About

Systems engineer with a security first perspective.

I work with systems engineering, cybersecurity, and backend architecture. My focus is on designing infrastructure that is observable, resilient to failure, and easy for teams to operate over time.

From homelab clusters and containerized developer images to network topologies and secure access patterns, I build systems that feel as polished as product experiences not just servers in a rack.
